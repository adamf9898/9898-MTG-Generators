# SKILL: Card Balancing and Playtesting

## Description
A personal skill to review, balance, and playtest custom Magic: The Gathering cards for fairness and fun.

## Workflow

1. Analyze card stats, abilities, and mana cost
2. Compare to similar Scryfall cards
3. Suggest adjustments for balance
4. Optionally simulate play scenarios

## Example Prompts
- "Balance this custom card for fair play."
- "Playtest a batch of new cards."
